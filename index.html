About Cutrico

Founded in 1984, Cutrico Ltd is a market leader in the supply, installation and maintenance of heating, ventilation and air-conditioning equipment in Malta and Gozo.

Our focus is quality products, expert installation, and dependable after-sales service — so your system performs well year after year.
